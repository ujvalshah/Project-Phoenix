# Community & Content Guidelines

## Our Mission

Nuggets is a platform for curating, organizing, and sharing valuable content. These guidelines establish the standards and expectations for all community members to ensure a respectful, safe, and productive environment.

## Content Standards

### What Belongs on Nuggets

We encourage users to share content that is:

- **Informative:** Educational articles, tutorials, how-to guides, and industry insights
- **Relevant:** News, analysis, and commentary on topics of genuine interest
- **Well-organized:** Content with clear titles, appropriate tags, and meaningful descriptions
- **Properly attributed:** Content that credits original sources and respects intellectual property

### Prohibited Content

The following types of content are not allowed on the platform:

- **Illegal content:** Any material that violates applicable laws or regulations
- **Hate speech:** Content that promotes hatred, violence, or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- **Harassment:** Targeted abuse, threats, intimidation, or bullying directed at individuals or groups
- **Spam:** Repetitive, low-quality, or irrelevant content posted for promotional purposes
- **Misinformation:** Deliberately false or misleading content intended to deceive
- **Explicit content:** Pornographic, sexually explicit, or gratuitously violent material
- **Malware:** Content containing or linking to malicious software, phishing attempts, or scams
- **Personal information:** Sharing private information about others without their consent (doxxing)
- **Impersonation:** Pretending to be another person, brand, or organization

## Community Behavior

### Be Respectful

- Treat all community members with courtesy and respect
- Engage in constructive discussions and provide helpful feedback
- Acknowledge different perspectives and experiences
- Avoid personal attacks, even in disagreements

### Be Honest

- Share content accurately and in good faith
- Disclose affiliations or conflicts of interest when relevant
- Do not manipulate engagement metrics or game the platform
- Report content that violates these guidelines

### Be Responsible

- Verify information before sharing, especially on sensitive topics
- Use appropriate tags and categories to help others find relevant content
- Respect the platform's features and use them as intended
- Keep your account secure and do not share your credentials

## Content Organization

### Tags and Categorization

- Use relevant, accurate tags that describe your content
- Avoid misleading or irrelevant tags designed to increase visibility
- Follow existing tag conventions when available
- Keep tag names concise and descriptive

### Collections

- Create collections with clear, descriptive names
- Organize content thoughtfully to provide value to others
- Public collections should be genuinely useful to the community
- Do not create duplicate or near-duplicate collections

## Intellectual Property

- Only share content you have the right to distribute
- Credit original authors and sources
- Respect copyright, trademarks, and other intellectual property rights
- When linking to external content, ensure the link serves an informational purpose
- See our Copyright Notice for detailed information on IP policies

## Reporting and Enforcement

### How to Report Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

- Use the platform's built-in reporting feature to flag the content
- Provide specific details about the violation when submitting a report
- Do not engage with or amplify violating content

### Enforcement Actions

We may take the following actions in response to guideline violations:

- **Warning:** A notification that content or behavior violates guidelines
- **Content removal:** Removing specific content that violates policies
- **Temporary suspension:** Restricting account access for a defined period
- **Permanent ban:** Terminating account access for severe or repeated violations

The severity of enforcement depends on:

- The nature and severity of the violation
- Whether the violation is a first offense or a pattern
- The impact on other users and the community
- Whether the user acted in good faith

### Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was made in error, you may appeal through the feedback feature on the platform. Include relevant context and explain why you believe the action should be reconsidered.

## Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these guidelines as our community grows and evolves. Material changes will be communicated through the platform. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated guidelines.

## Governing Law

These Community & Content Guidelines shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the applicable jurisdiction.

## Questions

If you have questions about these guidelines or need clarification on what is or isn't allowed, please reach out through the feedback feature on our platform.
