# Cookie Policy

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to site operators.

## How We Use Cookies

Nuggets ("the platform") uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

- **Authentication tokens:** To keep you signed in and manage your session securely
- **Security cookies:** To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse
- **Preference cookies:** To remember your settings such as dark/light mode theme preference

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your choices:

- **View mode preference:** Remembering your preferred layout (grid, masonry, or utility view)
- **Filter and sort settings:** Preserving your selected categories, tags, and sort order
- **Scroll position:** Restoring your feed position when navigating back
- **Collapsed/expanded states:** Remembering UI element states across sessions

### Analytics and Performance Cookies

We may use analytics tools to understand how the platform is used:

- **Error tracking:** Capturing and reporting errors to improve stability (e.g., Sentry)
- **Performance monitoring:** Measuring page load times and API response times
- **Usage patterns:** Understanding which features are used most to prioritize improvements

## Local Storage and Session Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage and session storage for:

- **Authentication data:** Securely storing access tokens and refresh tokens
- **User preferences:** Theme settings, view mode, and UI state
- **Cache data:** Temporarily caching API responses for faster page loads (managed by TanStack React Query)
- **Service worker data:** Supporting push notification functionality

## Third-Party Cookies

Our platform may set cookies from third-party services:

- **Embedded content:** YouTube videos, external media embeds, and linked content may set their own cookies
- **Error tracking:** Sentry may set cookies for error reporting and session replay
- **Analytics:** If enabled, analytics providers may set tracking cookies

We do not control third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective third-party privacy policies for more information.

## Cookie Duration

- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
- **Authentication tokens:** Expire based on server-configured timeframes, with automatic refresh when possible

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- View and delete existing cookies
- Block all cookies or only third-party cookies
- Set preferences for specific websites
- Receive notifications when cookies are set

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of the platform, including signing in.

### Clearing Local Storage

To clear locally stored data:

1. Open your browser's developer tools (usually F12)
2. Navigate to the Application or Storage tab
3. Clear local storage and session storage for our domain

Alternatively, signing out of your account will clear authentication-related stored data.

## Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. We currently do not respond to DNT signals, as there is no industry-wide standard for compliance. We will update this policy if a standard is established.
